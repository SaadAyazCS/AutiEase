# Privacy Policy for AutiEase

**LAST UPDATED: July 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy for AutiEase ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you download and use our mobile application (**AutiEase**).

AutiEase is an educational support mobile application for children on the Autism Spectrum (ASD) that provides parent-guided developmental planners, interactive learning games, and a secure communication consulting channel connecting parents with clinical therapists.

---

## SECTION 1: Privacy Policy for Parents & Caregivers

This section applies to Parents, Caregivers, and users registering child profiles on the platform.

### 1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

#### Personal information you disclose to us:
* **Standard Identifiers:** Names (parent and child first names), email addresses, passwords, phone numbers (mobile wallets for payment verification).
* **Sensitive Data:** Health data (child's developmental milestones and practice performance records), personal data from a known child (first name and age), and social security numbers or other government identifiers (where applicable).
* **Payment Data:** SafePay is used to process your payment credentials. We do not store or collect your payment card details directly. SafePay's policy is available at `https://getsafepay.pk/legal/content/privacy`.

#### Application Permissions Required:
* **Camera access:** To upload profile pictures.
* **Microphone access:** Used for speech practice games and speech-to-text recognition.
* **Storage access:** To upload local documents and photos.
* **Push Notifications:** To send chat messages and schedule updates.

#### Information automatically collected:
* **Log and Usage Data:** Firebase Analytics automatically records app interactions, device version specifications, page views, and crash reports.

### 2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process your information to:
* Facilitate account registration, logins, and profile validation.
* Deliver and facilitate the delivery of developmental planners and games.
* Enable secure chat messaging and coordination with independent therapists.
* Enforce app safety, respond to reports, and prevent fraud.
* Identify usage trends and improve user experience.

## SECTION 2: Privacy Policy for Professional Therapists & Specialists

This section applies to professionals registering therapist accounts and listing their profiles on the platform.

### 1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

#### Personal and professional information you disclose to us:
* **Standard Identifiers:** Name, email address, password, phone number.
* **Professional Details:** Bio, years and months of experience, clinical specializations, and uploaded qualification certificates (PDF).
* **Sensitive Data:** CNIC (National ID Card Number) for professional verification, and bank account details / IBAN (for payout withdrawals).
* **Chat Logs:** Transcripts of secure communication with parent clients.

#### Application Permissions Required:
* **Camera access:** To upload profile pictures.
* **Storage access:** To upload qualification certificates and files.
* **Push Notifications:** To receive real-time messages and booking updates.

#### Information automatically collected:
* **Log and Usage Data:** Firebase Analytics automatically records app interactions, device version specifications, page views, and crash reports.

### 2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

We process your information to:
* Verify identity, licensing, and professional credentials before listing on the directory.
* Provide a public consulting profile for parents to discover.
* Manage appointment scheduling and slot bookings.
* Transfer accumulated subscription earnings to your bank account or mobile wallet.
* Maintain security, safety checks, and moderation policies.

## SECTION 3: General Policies (Applicable to All Users)

### 3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We only share information with the following third-party categories:
* Cloud Computing Services (Firebase)
* Data Analytics Services (Firebase Analytics)
* Data Storage Service Providers (Firestore & Storage)
* Payment Processors (SafePay)
* User Account Registration & Authentication Services (Firebase Auth, Google Sign-In)

**Your data is NOT sold, shared, or rented to third-party ad networks.**

### 4. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS
Your data is stored in the United States on Firebase servers. We adhere to the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure secure data transfer safeguards.

### 5. RETENTION OF INFORMATION
We keep your personal information only as long as you maintain an active account with us. When you request account deletion, all personal data is permanently wiped from our databases.

### 6. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?
We implement industry-standard organizational and technical security measures. However, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

### 7. WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS?
You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time.
